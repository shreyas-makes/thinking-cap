## Shortlist

- `04` is the strongest readable block logo direction.
- `08` is the most compact option for a narrow terminal header.
- `10` feels the most like a product wordmark rather than a font sample.
- `07` is worth exploring if you want a more cut-out, industrial brand feel.
- `20` is the strongest of the second batch for a final logo treatment.
- `11` and `17` are good if you want a denser, more machine-like header.
